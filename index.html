This is ISERC I/O!

ISERC I/O is a monthly seminar hosted by students of ISERC which provides a platform for students to present and exchange their summer research experience and to participate in an engaging and probative discussion of the ideas presented. In this event, students of ISERC are fully committed to use ISERC I/O as a mean to share knowledge and bring new approaches to advancing entrepreneurship among younger generations.
